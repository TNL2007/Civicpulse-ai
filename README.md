## 📖 Overview

CivicPulse AI is an intelligent civic engagement platform designed to bridge the gap between citizens and municipal authorities through AI-powered automation.

Citizens can instantly report civic issues such as potholes, overflowing garbage bins, damaged streetlights, water leakages, road damages, and public infrastructure failures by uploading images along with issue descriptions.

The platform leverages Artificial Intelligence to automatically identify the issue type, estimate severity, detect duplicate reports, assign priority scores, and route complaints to the appropriate authority while enabling community verification and transparent status tracking.

The goal is to improve urban governance by making civic issue reporting faster, smarter, and more transparent.

---

# 🎯 Problem Statement

Urban infrastructure issues often remain unresolved because:

- Citizens don't know where to report issues.
- Reports are duplicated multiple times.
- Authorities receive incomplete information.
- Prioritization is manual.
- Citizens have little visibility into complaint progress.

CivicPulse AI solves these challenges using AI-driven automation and community participation.

---

# 💡 Solution

Our platform provides an end-to-end workflow for civic issue management.

1. Citizens upload an image and description.
2. AI classifies the issue.
3. AI estimates severity.
4. Duplicate reports are identified.
5. Location is mapped.
6. Priority score is generated.
7. Complaint is routed to the correct authority.
8. Community verifies authenticity.
9. Authorities update progress.
10. Citizens track resolution in real time.
